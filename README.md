# AI Governance Risk Assessment – HR AI Screening System (NIST AI RMF Case Study)

##  Overview
This project demonstrates how the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) can be applied to assess and manage risks in a real-world AI use case.

The system analyzed is a hypothetical AI-powered resume screening tool used to assist HR teams in candidate selection.

The objective is to identify, measure, and manage risks such as bias, lack of explainability, privacy concerns, and overreliance on AI outputs.

---

##  Use Case
The AI system:
- Analyzes candidate resumes
- Assigns a suitability score
- Helps HR prioritize candidates

Important:
The system is designed to **support human decision-making**, not replace it. Final decisions must be made by human reviewers.

---

## Why This Use Case is High-Risk
AI in hiring directly impacts individuals’ opportunities and livelihoods.

Key risks include:
- Discrimination and bias
- Lack of transparency in decision-making
- Privacy violations
- Overreliance on automated outputs

---

## Framework Used
This project is structured based on the **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)**:

- **GOVERN** → Policies, roles, accountability, and oversight  
- **MAP** → Context, stakeholders, risks, and use-case definition  
- **MEASURE** → Evaluation of system performance and trustworthiness  
- **MANAGE** → Risk prioritization, mitigation, and monitoring
