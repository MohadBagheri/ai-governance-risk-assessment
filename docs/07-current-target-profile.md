# AI RMF Current vs Target Profile – HR AI Screening System

| Area | Current State | Target State | Gap | Action |
|------|--------------|-------------|-----|--------|
| Governance | Human oversight and control measures are defined, providing a basic governance foundation. | A formal governance framework with clearly defined roles, accountability mechanisms, and documented AI policies. | Lack of formal role definitions, accountability structures, and governance policies. | Define roles (e.g., AI system owner, HR reviewer, compliance), establish AI governance policies, and implement audit and logging mechanisms to ensure accountability and traceability. |

| MEASURE | Key risks are identified, but no formal measurement framework is defined. | A comprehensive measurement framework with quantitative and qualitative metrics to assess performance, fairness, explainability, and system behavior over time. | Absence of defined metrics and monitoring processes. | Define performance, fairness, and explainability metrics, and implement continuous monitoring mechanisms. |
| MANAGE | Control measures and human oversight practices are defined, providing initial risk management mechanisms. | A structured risk management framework with defined incident response processes, risk prioritization, and residual risk documentation. | Lack of incident response process, risk prioritization, and residual risk documentation. | Establish an incident response process, implement risk prioritization based on impact and likelihood, and document residual risks. |
